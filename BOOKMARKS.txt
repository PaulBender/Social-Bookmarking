The following list is some of the most common bookmarking sites you can add. Just copy and paste to the Social Bookmarking page of the Admin CP.
The icons are already included in the images/bookmarks folder:

Digg
http://digg.com/submit?phrase=2&url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/digg.png

del.icio.us
https://del.icio.us/post?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/delicious.png

StumbleUpon
http://www.stumbleupon.com/badge?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/stumbleupon.png

Facebook
https://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/facebook.png

Google
https://www.google.com/bookmarks/mark?op=edit&bkmk={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/google.png

Reddit
https://www.reddit.com/submit?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/reddit.png

Twitter
https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text={title} {url}
images/bookmarks/twitter.png

LinkedIn
https://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/linkedin.png

Blogger
https://www.blogger.com/blog-this.g?u={url}&n={title}
images/bookmarks/blogger.png

Fark
https://www.fark.com/submit?new_url={url}
images/bookmarks/fark.png

Tumblr
https://www.tumblr.com/widgets/share/tool?canonicalUrl={url}
images/bookmarks/tumblr.png

Pinterest
http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url={url}
images/bookmarks/pinterest.png
